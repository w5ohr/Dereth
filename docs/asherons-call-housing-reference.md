# Asheron's Call Player Housing — Reference (researched 2026-07, asheron.fandom.com wiki)

## Dwelling types (canonical)
| | Apartment | Cottage | Villa | Mansion |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cost | 100,000 py + 1 Writ of Refuge | 300,000 py + 1 Writ + trophy | 2,000,000 py + 5 Writs + trophy | 10,000,000 py + 20 Writs + trophies |
| Level req | 20+ | 20+ | 35+ | 50+ AND Monarch rank 6+ |
| Maintenance | 10,000 py / 3 mo | 30,000 py / mo | 100,000 py + 2 Writs / mo | 1,000,000 py + 10 Writs / mo |
| Hooks | 3 (2 wall, 1 floor) | 50 (25 usable) | 70 (50 usable) | 100 (all usable) |
| Storage chests | 1 | 1 | 2 | 5 |

- Trophy per settlement (Ahr-Zona: Sclavus Tongue / Golem Jo; Bucolic Villas: Carenzi Stalker Pelt; Far Claw: Ivory Gromnie Tooth).
- One dwelling per account; 15 days played required; 30-day rebuy timer after abandon (apartments exempt).
- Buy at the **Covenant Crystal** outside each dwelling (accepts pyreals or trade notes — villa = 8× MMD). Crystal shows owner name after sale.
- Access: /house guest add (128 max), /house open|close|boot, storage permission; recall: /house recall (/hor), mansion/alleg recall (/hom /hoa).
- Writs of Refuge: housing currency from Arcanum agents (trophy turn-ins/quests).

## Settlements (authentic)
- Cottage settlements: Mirthless Dale (23.2N 42.3W, near Danby's Outpost), Ahr-Zona (21.6S 18.5E, near Al-Arqas, 14 cottages), Alfreth Ridge, Bright Blade, Wisp Lake, Yukikaze. 2-14 dwellings around a shared courtyard (fountain, benches, lampposts).
- Villa settlements: Bucolic Villas (41.6N 15.4E, near Glenden Wood, 2 villas + fountain), Far Claw Villas (54.0N 58.9E, near Bandit Castle, 11 villas), Tarn Vinara (Al-Arqas), East Al-Jalima, Beach Pass.
- Apartments ("Residential Quarters"): dungeon halls of tiny units off a Great Hall; Sanctum Residential Halls (Cragstone 25.5N 48.7E, Hebian-To, Zaikhal) first; then Atrium/Oriel/Haven/Victory. 3 hooks + 1 chest per unit.
- Settlement portal hubs near major towns became a beloved fast-travel network.
